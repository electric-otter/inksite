Rules for Creating a Website

  • Ensure your content is original and does not infringe on copyright laws.
  • Provide clear and accurate information about your business or services.
  • Include a privacy policy and terms of service to inform users about data handling.
  • Make your website accessible to all users, including those with disabilities.
  • Use secure protocols (HTTPS) to protect user data.
  • Follow the laws and regulations of the United States, or the country you reside in, to ensure your website is compliant with local legal standards.
